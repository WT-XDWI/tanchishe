# Appendix E: Export Control & Data Protection Compliance Reference

> **Note**: This appendix provides compliance references for the latest regulatory developments in export control and data protection. **It is not contractually binding** — it serves as a practical compliance guide.

---

## 1. Export Control

| # | Regulation / Document | Effective / Updated | Key Points |
| :-: | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Export Control Law of the PRC** | Dec 1, 2020 | Establishes the basic export control system, covering controlled items, controlled activities, licensing, and extraterritorial jurisdiction |
| 2 | **Regulations on the Export Control of Dual-Use Items** | 2024 | Refines the scope and control measures for dual-use items |
| 3 | **Dual-Use Items Export Control List** | Updated Oct 2025 | Specifies controlled items — licensees should periodically verify |
| 4 | **Foreign Trade Law** | Revised Mar 1, 2026 | Adds new export control-related institutional arrangements |
| 5 | **New Customs Declaration Rules** | Apr–Jun 2026 | General Administration of Customs added two new declaration fields: "Restricted Control Identification Code" and "Restricted Control Declaration Elements" |
| 6 | **U.S. Export Administration Regulations (EAR) & EU Sanctions Regulations** | — | Must also be complied with if the licensee is in or involves relevant jurisdictions |

> **Tip**: Licensees should regularly check for updates from the Ministry of Commerce (www.mofcom.gov.cn) and the General Administration of Customs (www.customs.gov.cn).

---

## 2. Data Protection

| # | Regulation / Standard | Effective / Published | Key Points |
| :-: | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL)** | Nov 1, 2021 | Establishes personal information processing rules, regulates automated decision-making and cross-border transfers |
| 2 | **Data Security Law** | Sep 1, 2021 | Establishes a data classification and grading protection system, defines security obligations for important data processors |
| 3 | **Cybersecurity Law** | Jun 1, 2017 | Requires network operators to fulfill cybersecurity protection obligations |
| 4 | **GB/T 46068-2025** — Data Security Technology — Security Certification Requirements for Cross-Border Personal Information Processing Activities | Mar 1, 2026 | Technical standard for personal information export certification |
| 5 | **Personal Information Export Certification Measures** | Published Oct 2025 | Refines the personal information export certification mechanism |
| 6 | **Provisions on Facilitating and Regulating Cross-Border Data Flows** | — | Clarifies circumstances exempt from declaration |

### Three Compliance Paths for Personal Information Exports

1. **Declare for Data Export Security Assessment**
2. **Conclude a Standard Contract for Personal Information Export**
3. **Obtain Personal Information Protection Certification**

> **Tip**: Licensees should ensure a lawful basis for data processing activities and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard data security. While the above national standards are not legally mandatory, they serve as best-practice references for compliance.
